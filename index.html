FAQ

Answers for your active life

Explore our most common questions about customising your recovery, using our tools, and making movement more enjoyable every day.

How does shake-to-program work?

Just give the massage ball a shake in the recording mode—our sensors read your unique motion and instantly create a custom vibration beat. It’s your rhythm, your way.

Can I personalise the look of my massage ball?

Yes! Mix and match silicone skins and centre buttons to suit your mood or style. You can even design your own set of skins. The skin is interchangeable, dedicated for self-expression.

Is it quiet enough for shared spaces?

Absolutely. The advanced motor keeps things powerful but whisper-quiet, so you can recover at home, at your desk, or anywhere—without bothering anyone. We are well below 50 dB.

Who can use these recovery tools?

Anyone who wants to move better and feel good—athletes, creators, desk workers, and everyone in between. No experience needed, just curiosity.

What’s coming with the app?

Soon you’ll get guided routines and community challenges—all designed to make recovery smarter, more social, and more fun.

Can I recharge my massage ball?

Absolutely. Just plug in with a USB-C cable. You can charge with your laptop or power bank, in your car, or at a wall socket.
